Projects

SameRoute

Smart mobility platform for schools and municipalities

Startup
Sameroute is a smart mobility platform designed for schools and municipalities to make student transportation safer, more efficient, and less stressful for families. The app connects parents who can act as drivers with other families in their area, allowing them to share rides to and from school. By organizing trips in advance or joining nearby active routes in real time, Sameroute reduces traffic congestion, saves time, and optimizes transport resources. With a strong focus on safety, transparency, and community trust, Sameroute enables guardians to manage their children's rides, track journeys, and ensure reliable daily transportation — all in one place.

StackersProg

Specialized formwork slab optimization software for construction professionals

Construction Software
StackersProg is a specialized construction software built for formwork slab optimization. Designed for builders and construction professionals, it streamlines the planning and calculation of concrete slab frameworks — saving time, reducing material waste, and improving accuracy on site. Trusted by construction teams across Latvia, StackersProg simplifies one of the most complex parts of the building process, giving professionals a reliable and efficient tool to work with.

Channy

AI-powered autonomous video creation and publishing platform

AI App
Channy is an intelligent video creation platform that automates the entire content production workflow. Upload your own footage or set search parameters, and Channy's AI agents handle everything — autonomous scene detection, smart clip extraction, emotion-driven editing via the built-in Remotion engine, and export-ready short-form content. No manual editing, no technical setup. Users connect their social media platform API keys to enable direct publishing to YouTube, TikTok, and more. A subscription plan funds the AI editing costs, so you just choose your settings and let Channy do the work — on mobile or desktop.
